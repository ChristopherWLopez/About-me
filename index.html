About me

Contact:

My name is Christopher Lopez, I am 33 years old and I grew up in the greater Los Angeles area, I was raised primarily By my grandparents (while my dad toured as a drummer) in Compton Ca, There I picked up the guitar to be able to play music with my brother who is also a drummer. Eventually we moved from Compton over to Whittier ca (closer to east LA than OC). I did complete my basic schooling and did take SOME college, My early studies were based around majoring in “music composition” … Life hit hard and I eventually stopped going to school. For the past 14 years I have worked at Trader joes while occasionally gigging (around LA) as a drummer, I suppose that would be considered a “profession” of mine. In recent years I have managed to narrow in on my goals and what I would like accomplish. I would love to be able to become financially stable enough to buy a home somewhere with a lot of land and take care of animals ( I love animals and would love to open a rescue) I specifically love the smaller animals that some consider “pests” like squirrels, raccoons, opossums… however I would absolutely love to take care of larger animals. Besides that I want to have my own recording studio where I can record an album with my dad and my brother… I realize that would be an amazing life goal for me.
